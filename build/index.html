How I work

My process is an evolved practice that I have used over a number of years, ensuring continuous improvement using lean and agile methodologies.

1

Planning

This is arguably the most important stage in getting your website built. What we discuss and plan here sets the stage for the whole project. I'll go through your requirements and discuss your goals, audience and the features you would like on the website.

2

Design

Here I will gather the information from our planning and work on a site structure using wireframes before creating a visual representation of your new website. The wireframes and mock ups then undergo a review and approval cycle until we are all happy with the design.

3

Develop

The development stage is where the programming takes place and your website gets built, populated with content and tested. My main tools are HTML5, CSS3, JS, Bootstrap but I can also build and manage sites in and Wordpress if required while keeping you in the loop.

4

Launch

This is where your website gets transferred to the live server and becomes available for public viewing! Once live we will run through some final testing and cross-browser checks to ensure cross-browser compatibility and a smooth transition from development server to live server.
